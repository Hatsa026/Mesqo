From the pod

Tastes like coffee. Won't keep you up.

It comes from the mesquite pod, not a coffee bean. Naturally sweet, a bit nutty, with a soft caramel thing going on and no bitterness to hide behind. There was never any caffeine in it to take out, so it won't decide when your day is over. Have one at four. Have one after dinner. Have another if you feel like it.

Who we are

Two brothers, two cities, one cup.

We are two brothers, one in Amsterdam and one in Madrid. We came across roasted mesquite, brewed it like coffee, and ended up liking it a lot more than we expected.

Until now, coffee without the caffeine mostly meant decaf. And decaf is just coffee with the caffeine stripped back out, often with chemical solvents and a fair bit of processing. MESQO skips all of that. The mesquite pod never had any caffeine to begin with, so there is nothing to remove and nothing to add. It is simply roasted, ground and brewed. Natural the whole way through.

We liked it too much to keep it to ourselves.

Wouter & Wisse
