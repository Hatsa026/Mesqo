Why Mesqo

What's actually
in it.

Mesqo is a different category entirely - a roasted mesquite espresso that happens to be naturally caffeine-free. Here's what's in the pod.

Naturally caffeine-free
Not decaffeinated - there's simply no caffeine in the mesquite pod to begin with. No solvents, no extraction, no trace amounts.
High in dietary fibre
Mesquite pods are roughly 25% fibre by composition. That's high for any roasted ingredient - and something coffee and decaf can't offer at all.
Mineral content
Contains calcium, magnesium, potassium, zinc and iron - naturally present in the pod, not added in.
Low acidity
Gentler on the stomach than coffee. If acidity is the reason you've cut coffee out, mesquite espresso gives you the ritual back.
A long nutritional history
Mesquite pods have been eaten as a staple food for thousands of years. Traditionally valued for steady, sustained energy.
